Bitcontract intends to change the way legal or physical persons conclude agreements:


1. Contracts are no longer in paper form but in electronical form, stored in our secured database.
2. Parties of the contract no longer have to meet or to send the contract one to another, they simply have to login on the site and to agree on the proposed contract.
3. Contracts shall be extremely simplified. This means usually of 1 page, maximum of 5 pages.
4. Bitcontract is applicable to any kind of relation you need to regulate by means of a contract.
5. Our contract database combined with our team of specialist (available 24/7) shall make it easier than ever to secure your rights and obligations.
6. Bitcontracts are currently enforceable in US and Europe. We are working hard to ensure world wide enforceability.

FAQ

Yes, it is. Our core speciality is law. The Bitcontracts are a simple expression in the electronic world of our speciality.
